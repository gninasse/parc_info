# Initial Concept
A modular Laravel application for IT Asset Management (Parc Informatique), Human Resources (GRH), and Organizational tracking.

# Product Guide

## Target Audience
- IT Administrators managing hardware and software assets.
- HR Personnel managing employee records and roles.
- Organization Managers overseeing departments and services.

## Core Features
- **Asset Management (ParcInfo):** Tracking hardware, workstations, software, and assignments.
- **Human Resources (GRH):** Employee directory, roles, and permissions management.
- **Organization:** Service and department hierarchy, user assignments.
- **Core Security:** Activity logging, role-based access control (RBAC).

## Goals
- Centralize IT asset and HR data in a single system.
- Provide a clear, modular, and maintainable platform.
- Ensure strict security and activity tracking for all actions.
