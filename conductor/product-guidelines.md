# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone:** Professional & Direct. The language should be clear, concise, and suitable for enterprise tools, ensuring instructions and feedback are easily understood by both IT and HR personnel.

## UX Principles
- **Data-Dense Views:** Prioritize layouts that display maximum information efficiently (e.g., comprehensive data tables). This is critical for IT administrators who need a broad overview of assets and HR personnel managing large employee lists.

## Visual Style & Branding
- **UI Approach:** Admin Dashboard (AdminLTE). Utilize the established AdminLTE assets to maintain a classic, familiar administrative feel, ensuring consistency and leveraging existing frontend structures.
